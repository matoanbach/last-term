# Proposal for Short Story & Identity Memoir (5%)

**Course:** LSO 100: Canadian Short Story

## Due Date

Please refer to the Course Schedule and Blackboard for the submission deadline.

## Weight

5% of the final grade

## Submission

Submit your completed proposal template through the designated Blackboard submission folder.

## Purpose

This assignment serves as a prelude to the major Short Story & Identity Memoir assignment (20%) that you will complete later in the course. It is intended to help you begin planning, reflecting, and organizing your ideas before writing the full memoir.

You are not writing the memoir itself at this stage. Instead, you will submit a proposal outlining the literary text you have chosen, the aspect of identity you wish to explore, and your preliminary ideas for connecting the short story to your own experiences and reflections.

The feedback you receive on this proposal should guide and strengthen your work on the final Short Story & Identity Memoir (20%), which will be submitted in Week 8, following the Study Week.

## Instructions

This assignment is a proposal only. Think of it as a blueprint for your final memoir rather than the memoir itself.

Rather than writing a traditional paragraph-style response, complete the planning template below. The purpose of this format is to help you organize your thinking, receive feedback, and develop a clear direction for your final memoir.

Choose one short story from the course readings completed up to Week 7 as the foundation for your Identity Memoir.
