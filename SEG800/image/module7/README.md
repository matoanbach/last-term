# Intro to Image processing
## Basic Definitions
- Image: A 2-dimensional function, f(x, y), where x and y are spatial (plan) coordinates, and the amplitude of f at any pair of coordiates (x, y) is called the intensity of gray level of the image at that point.
- Digital Image: An image for which x, y, and the intensity values of f are all finite, discret quantities.
- Pixel: Each element of a digital image - with a specific (x, y) location and thus f(x, y) intensity. Short for `picture element`.
- Digital Image Processing: Processing a digital image with a digital computer.
