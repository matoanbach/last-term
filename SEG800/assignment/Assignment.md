## Assignment Topic

1. Choose three of the following topics in order of preference:

   - **[DSP]** Notch Filters
   - **[DSP]** Comb Filters
   - **[DSP]** Inverse Systems and Deconvolution
   - **[DIP]** Wavelet Image Transform
   - **[DIP]** Video Compression (H.264 or H.265)
   - **[DIP]** Using fuzzy techniques for intensity transformation and spatial filtering

2. Email both professors with your ranked preferences and group number, and CC the other members of your group. Selection will be based on the email timestamp or random assignment if there are conflicts.

3. Get confirmation of the topic your group will work on. Your topic is confirmed when it is posted on the assignment page.

4. Research the topic thoroughly so that you are able to teach it.

## Deliverables

1. **Tutorial Document — 30 marks**  
   Create a 5–10-page tutorial for your classmates on the topic. It will be shared with the class.

2. **Sample Code — 30 marks**  
   Create a workshop or code samples demonstrating an implementation or application related to your topic. The material will be shared with the class.

3. **Group Presentation — 30 marks**  
   Create a video that teaches the concept. Each group has a maximum of 15 minutes, approximately 5 minutes per person. Everyone in the group must present. In addition to covering the required content, make the video engaging and easy to understand. It will be shared with the class.

4. **Peer Review — 10 marks**  
   Evaluate group presentations according to the instructions given in class. You will be marked based on the quality of your reviews.
