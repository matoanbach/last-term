# Lab 2 - Question 3 (Scalability and Performance Analysis)

## Metrics

Speedup:

$$
Speedup(p) = \frac{T_s}{T_p}
$$

Efficiency:

$$
Efficiency(p) = \frac{Speedup(p)}{p}
$$

Weak-scaling result: wall time increased as process count increased, showing communication, synchronization, memory pressure, and oversubscription overhead.

## Communication Behavior

- `MPI_Scatter` introduces communication overhead before computation.
- `MPI_Reduce` introduces synchronization cost after computation.
- Efficiency decreases at higher process counts because overhead grows relative to useful work.
- The 16-process and 32-process cases oversubscribe the 8-core system.
